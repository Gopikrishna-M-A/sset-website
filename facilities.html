FACILITIES

HOSTELS

Separate hostels for men and women are available in the campus. The Men's Hostels can accommodate 489 students and the Ladies' Hostel can accommodate 390 students. There is a separate hostel for first year boys inside the campus. Security personnel have been deployed at the hostels round the clock.

HOSTEL FACILITIES
  • Water purifiers for constant supply of safe drinking water
  • Room with cable TV
  • Computer Centre with Internet Facility
  • Indoor games like table tennis, carroms and chess
  • Backup power using sound proof diesel generator
  • Magazines/newspaper in every block
  • Badminton courts, Geyser for Hot water supply
  • First aid facility
  • Top rainwater harvesting structures for water recharging

TRANSPORTATION

A fleet of 22 college buses is provided for students and staff and plies to Thoppumpadi, Tripunithura, Kakkanad, Ernakulam, Kodungallur , Perumbavoor and Thrissur which covers all pick up points of the city and scheduled as per the convenience of class timings for staff and students. Separate college buses are provided for first year students.

CANTEEN

A state of the art cafeteria with a most modern centralized food production facility is also in place which serves both vegetarian and non-vegetarian (Halal meat) items. It has an area of 1300 m2 which is spacious enough to accommodate 500 students comfortably at a time. The canteen timings are staggered for first and senior years so that the new students are free to move in the campus.

COLLEGE STORE

Stationery and books required by the students are made available through the College Store. Reprographic Centre attached to the store caters the requirementsof photocopying, scanning, binding reports etc
