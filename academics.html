The SCMS School of Engineering & Technology (SSET), is the first engineering college in the self-finance scheme to offer basic branches like Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and Electrical Engineering right from the first year of establishment of the institution. Over the years it has added more courses in Electronics and Communication, Computer Science, Automobile Engineering at the B.Tech level and MCA and M.Tech at the post graduate level.

SSET has a total sanctioned intake capacity of 660 students in six branches of B.Tech, 132 students in six branches of M.Tech and 60 students of MCA. SSET, already recognized as a centre of excellence, offers courses in traditional engineering as well as technology-based disciplines.

B.Tech
SSET offers B.Tech programmes with an intake capacity as given below:
B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering - 180
B.Tech Electronics & Communication Engineering - 60
B.Tech Mechanical Engineering - 60
B.Tech Electrical & Electronics Engineering - 30
B.Tech Civil Engineering - 60
B.Tech in Civil and Environmental Engineering - 30
B.Tech Automobile Engineering - 60
B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering(Data Science) - 60
B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering(Artificial Intelligence) - 30

All the above B.Tech programmes are of four year duration (eight semesters). End semester examinations are conducted by the
APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU), Trivandrum.



M.Tech
SSET offers M.Tech programmes with an intake capacity as given below:
CIVIL ENGINEERING
M.Tech in Computer Aided Structural Engineering - 18
M.Tech in Environmental Engineering - 24
MECHANICAL ENGINEERING
M.Tech in Production and Industrial Engineering -18
COMPUTER SCIENCE & ENGINEERING
M.Tech in Computer Science and Information System -18
ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING
M.Tech in VLSI & Embedded System Engineering -18
M.Tech in Communication Engineering -18

M.Tech programmes are of 2 year duration and are affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU), Trivandrum.



MCA
SSET offers MCA with an intake capacity as given below:
MASTER OF COMPUTER APPLICATIONS (MCA) - 30

Master of Computer Applications (MCA) programme is of 2 years duration and is affiliated to
APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU), Trivandrum.
