CAMPUS OVERVIEW


The SSET has established state of the art facilities on a sprawling 29 acre campus at Karukutty in Ernakulam district.Salient features are beautiful village atmosphere with no air pollution,greenary surroundings ,Eco-friendly and efficient water harvesting and recycling systems.Buildings and play grounds are constructed with out affecting the greenery and natural landscape of the land.



The College Campus is located at Vidyanagar, Palissery, Karukutty. It is 37 km from Kochi, 5 km East of NH-47 from Adlux international convention centre, Cochin


  • 9 km from Karukutty Railway Station. 20 km from Kochi International Airport.
  • 6 km from bus-stop at Karukutty on National Highway.
  • Bus Stop in front of college for Angamali and Chalakkudy
